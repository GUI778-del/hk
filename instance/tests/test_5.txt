The Student Union plays a vital role in enhancing campus life. To better serve the student body in the coming year, I would like to offer the following suggestions.
First, organizing more cultural events would be highly beneficial. Activities such as film festivals, art exhibitions, and music nights can broaden students’ horizons and provide much-needed relaxation. Second, expanding sports competitions and recreational leagues would encourage physical well-being and teamwork. Third, academic support should be strengthened through regular workshops and lecture series on skills like public speaking and career planning. Finally, creating more platforms for cross-departmental interaction—such as themed socials or club fairs—would help students build stronger connections across disciplines.
By implementing these suggestions, the Student Union can significantly enrich our university experience, making campus life more vibrant, supportive, and engaging for everyone.
